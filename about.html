OUR STORY &
PHILOSOPHY

abcdef was founded in 2018 with a singular mission: to redefine the narrative of Indian cuisine in San Francisco. Inspired by the royal kitchens of Lucknow and Hyderabad, our culinary team marries old-world slow-cooking techniques with modern culinary refinement.

We believe that spices are the music of food. Instead of overpowering the dish, spices should harmonize with the ingredients, highlighting their natural character. Our signature masalas are freshly ground in-house daily, using spices imported directly from artisanal farms across India.

Whether you're joining us for a celebratory dinner, a business lunch, or a casual cocktail at our brass-trimmed bar, we promise an environment of warmth, elegance, and unforgettable culinary craftsmanship.
